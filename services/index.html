Service 01

Aerial Mapping
& Survey.

Engineering-grade aerial data, captured by drone — delivered as files your team can drop straight into GIS, CAD, or BIM.

Drone-based surveys for agriculture, construction, mining, urban planning, and land management. We plan the flight, set ground control, capture the imagery, and deliver clean orthomosaics, terrain models, and 3D meshes — referenced and ready to use.

How it works 3 stages · From flight plan to deliverable
Stage 01

Plan & Capture

Site walk, flight planning, ground control, and multi-flight capture at the resolution your job needs.

  • Site & airspace planning
  • RTK ground control points
  • Multi-flight capture
  • Quality checks on-site
Stage 02

Process

Photogrammetric processing with geo-referencing and accuracy validated against your control points.

  • Photogrammetric pipeline
  • Geo-referencing
  • Accuracy validation
  • Cloud / dense point cloud
Stage 03

Deliver

Outputs delivered in the formats your engineers and analysts already work in.

  • Orthomosaics & DEMs
  • Contour maps
  • 3D mesh & textured models
  • GIS-ready exports
Inspection · Power · Pipelines · Towers
Service 02

Infrastructure
Inspection.

Power lines, pipelines, towers, and remote assets — inspected from the air, in less time and with better data than ground crews.

Routine drone inspections for high-voltage transmission, oil & gas pipelines, telecom towers, and industrial facilities. Safer than rope teams, faster than line crews, and capable of capturing visual, zoom, and thermal data in a single sortie — delivered as a geo-referenced inspection report your asset managers can act on.

How it works 3 stages · Survey to actionable report
Stage 01

Asset Survey

Pre-flight planning, asset cataloguing, and mission corridor mapping over your network.

  • Asset cataloguing
  • Corridor & route mapping
  • Risk & airspace planning
  • Operator briefing
Stage 02

Sensor Suite

The right payload for the asset — RGB, zoom, or thermal, captured in a single flight.

  • High-resolution RGB
  • Optical zoom
  • Thermal imaging
  • Geo-tagged capture
Stage 03

Report

Anomalies are tagged, ranked by severity, and pinned to coordinates your team can find on the ground.

  • Anomaly tagging
  • Severity ranking
  • Geo-referenced report
  • Re-inspection scheduling
FabLab · Drone engineering
Service 03

Custom Drone
Builds.

Bespoke airframes, payload integrations, and autonomy stacks — engineered around your mission profile.

When off-the-shelf drones don't fit the job, we engineer them. Our FabLab and engineering team designs, fabricates, and field-tests drones built around your operational requirements — whether that's a specialised payload, a longer-endurance airframe, or a fully custom autonomy stack.

How it works 3 stages · Spec to handover
Stage 01

Spec & Design

Requirements, mission profile, and airframe sizing — locked before a single part is cut.

  • Requirements gathering
  • Airframe sizing
  • Payload modelling
  • CAD & review
Stage 02

Fabricate

Built in-house — printing, machining, electronics integration, and firmware tuned for the mission.

  • 3D printing & machining
  • Electronics integration
  • Firmware & autonomy
  • Bench testing
Stage 03

Test & Deliver

Calibration, flight testing, operator handover, and ongoing support after the drone enters service.

  • Calibration
  • Flight test programme
  • Operator handover
  • Service & support
