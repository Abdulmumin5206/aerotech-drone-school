FabLab · Drone engineering
Program 02

Drone Building
with FabLab.

Learn how drones are designed, manufactured, assembled, and tested.

A practical engineering course — one complete pathway, not multiple levels. Students learn how drone components work together and build a real drone using FabLab tools and electronics.

Curriculum 3 modules · One complete pathway
Module 01

Design

How a drone is shaped, sized, and specified before a single part is cut.

  • Drone components & architecture
  • Frame & chassis design
  • CAD basics
  • Materials & manufacturing
Module 02

Fabricate & Assemble

Print, wire, and put it all together using real FabLab tools and electronics.

  • 3D printing & fabrication
  • Motors & propellers
  • ESCs, batteries & power
  • Flight controller & wiring
Module 03

Calibrate & Test

Tune the build, troubleshoot it, and prove it flies — safely.

  • Calibration basics
  • Bench & flight testing
  • Troubleshooting
  • Safety checks
Professional operation · Field training
Program 03

Official Drone
Pilot Training.

For adults preparing for legal and professional drone operation.

Designed for adults who want to operate drones professionally and understand drone regulations, safety, mission planning, and practical flight. Curriculum aligns with the regulatory framework being established for civil drone operation in Uzbekistan.

Training path 3 modules · Ground school to certified flight
Module 01

Regulations & Safety

The rules, airspace, and operator responsibilities every legal pilot must know.

  • Drone regulations
  • Airspace awareness
  • Flight safety
  • Operator responsibility
Module 02

Plan the Mission

Read the conditions, brief the flight, and reduce risk before takeoff.

  • Pre-flight checklist
  • Mission planning
  • Weather & environment
  • Risk assessment
Module 03

Fly & Certify

Practical training in the air, emergency procedures, and final assessment.

  • Practical flight training
  • Emergency procedures
  • Final assessment process
